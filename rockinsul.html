What is Rockinsul?

Rockinsul stone wool insulation products are engineered for superior thermal insulation, fire protection and acoustic performance. These products are manufactured using advanced technology to provide long lasting and reliable insulation solutions for industrial and building applications.

  • Excellent fire resistance
  • Superior sound absorption
  • Energy efficiency improvement
  • Durable and long service life

Rockinsul Products

LRB Mattress

Flexible stone wool insulation used for boilers, ducts and process piping systems.

  • Thermal insulation
  • Fire resistant
  • Sound absorption
RB Slab

Rigid insulation board used in industrial and building insulation applications.

  • High density
  • Fire safety
  • Dimensional stability
Pipe Section

  • Energy efficient
  • Sound absorption

  • Fire resistant
  • Acoustic absorption
