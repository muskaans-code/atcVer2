What is Glass Wool?

Glass wool insulation is produced by spinning molten glass into fine fibers that trap air and create highly effective insulation. It provides excellent thermal resistance, sound absorption and fire safety for buildings and industrial systems.

  • Excellent thermal insulation
  • High sound absorption
  • Lightweight material
  • Non-combustible and fire safe
